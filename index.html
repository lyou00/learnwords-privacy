Overview

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

LearnWords is designed to operate entirely offline. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details to use the app.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. This includes:

  • Learning Progress: Your word mastery levels and study statistics
  • Favorites: Words you've marked as favorites
  • Settings: Your app preferences (theme, language, etc.)
  • Quiz Results: Your test scores and performance history

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or social media services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

Internet Permission

The app may request internet permission for the following purpose only:

  • Text-to-Speech: To provide pronunciation for English words (using your device's built-in TTS engine)

No personal data is transmitted during this process.

Children's Privacy

LearnWords is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious about installing apps from unknown sources

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all related data from your device
  • Reset: You can reset your progress through the app's settings
  • Export: Your data is stored locally and can be backed up with your device backup

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
